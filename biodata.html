WELCOME TO NISA BIODATA

About Me

Hi there! My name is Nik Nur Ainun Nisa’ binti Nik Abd Razak, but you can call me Nisa. I was born on 1st December 2004, and I’m 20 years old. I live in Bukit Mertajam, Penang, where I enjoy the calm surroundings and vibrant local culture. Currently, I’m pursuing my studies at UiTM Kedah in Diploma in Information Management, and I’m in my final year.

Originally, my dream was to become a lawyer, but over time, I’ve discovered my true calling is to become a teacher. I believe that teaching is one of the most rewarding careers, and I’m excited to embark on this journey of helping and inspiring others.

I have a deep passion for music, and one of my favorite hobbies is listening to songs. One of my favorite songs is "One of the Girls" by The Weeknd, JENNIE & Lily Rose Depp. It's a source of inspiration and joy for me. You can listen to it through this link. This love for music has even inspired me to create a song for someone special in my life. Apart from that, I enjoy visiting state mosques across Malaysia and national mosques around the world. It’s fascinating to observe the unique themes and architectural beauty of every mosque I visit.

One of my life principles is: "Every art has a story, and every story deserves to be appreciated." I live by this principle because I believe that everything, whether it's a song, a meal, or a building, has meaning, and that meaning is worth celebrating. I consider myself a patient person who doesn’t give up easily. I believe that perseverance and a positive attitude are key to overcoming challenges in life.

Through this website, I hope you’ll get to know more about me, my journey, my passions, and the little things that make me who I am. Thank you for stopping by, and I hope you enjoy your time here!
